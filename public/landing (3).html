How It Works

From homework page
to practice session in seconds

Just take a photo of the homework and BrainWiz does the rest — no lesson planning, no prep work.

1
Upload or enter a topic
Snap a photo of homework, type in a theme like "Roman History," or enter a book title for English. BrainWiz reads it instantly.
2
Pick your difficulty
Choose Starter, Building, or Challenging — calibrated to the actual homework, not just grade level.
3
Practice and learn
8 personalized questions per session with instant feedback, visual aids, hints, and a full explanation after each answer.
4
Review and improve
Sessions are saved by topic. Come back to weak spots, mix multiple topics, or track progress over time.
